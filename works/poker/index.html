I'm Hoardin' It!
Fall 2023
Poker Card Design

Since studying abroad, I left nearly all of loved stuffed animals back at home. Though I urge to hoard more, I had to give up since moving and storing is too stressful handling too much of them. So compromising with my hoarding habits, I compacted my stuffed animals into a set of poker cards to keep them with me.
