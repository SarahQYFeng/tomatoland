Travel Guide
Fall 2024
Poster & Zine Design

Inspired by the road sign trend in China tourism, I designed two collection posters and a travel guide zine to reflect on the life of a viral trend. I subverted the typical slogan “I’m Missing You from (City Name)”, and the role that a typical road sign plays, and displayed it as a landscape in the zine.

Advised by Hammett Nurosi
